Humidity

The above scatter plot illustrates that a relationship between humidity and latitude is highly unlikely. The correlation (as shown in the two graphs below) was very weak. On the day of the analysis, the majority of cities reported a humidity greater than 60% and several had 100% humidity. Notice that the only locations that had humidities less than 20% were concentrated near 20 degrees north or south of the equator.
